Other

Author, year Title Notes
Maunder and Punt (2004) Standardizing catch and effort data: a review of recent approaches Good background on CPUE. Includes intro to catch data standardization, dealing with zeros and interactions, and selecting explanatory variables
Hsieh et al. (2008) Spatial analysis shows that fishing enhances the climatic sensitivity of marine fishes Analyzed spatial area of occupancy and index of spatial heterogeneity, and changes in distribution from cold to warm period. Used linear regression and a variety of nonparametric tests
Author, year Title
Hamel et al. (2023) Natural mortality: Theory, estimation and application in fishery stock assessment models
Kenchington (2014) Natural mortality estimators for information-limited fisheries
